What is Gentrification?

Gentrification is the process through which wealthier residents move in, improve housing, and draw new businesses, often displacing locals in the process, changing the character of a poor metropolitan neighborhood.

Where is this happening?

The top 5 places where gentrification happens are:

    1. San Francisco-Oakland
    2. Denver
    3. Boston
    4. Miami
    5. New Orleans
